
# Bandhavgarh national park travel guide

**Discover the Roar of India's Premier Tiger Reserve: A Comprehensive Travel Guide to Bandhavgarh National Park**

Located in the heart of Madhya Pradesh, Bandhavgarh National Park is one of the most coveted wildlife sanctuaries in India, a must-visit destination for any travel enthusiast, nature lover, and wildlife aficionado. Home to an estimated 80-100 majestic Bengal tigers, the park is a veritable haven for those seeking a thrilling encounter with the ultimate monarch of the wild. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will delve into the best time to visit Bandhavgarh, how to reach the park, the top places to visit, a day-wise itinerary, and essential travel tips and budgeting advice.

**Best Time to Visit**

Bandhavgarh National Park remains open for tourism from October 1st to June 30th, with the best time to visit the park falling between November and April. During these months, the park offers pleasant weather with crisp mornings, sunny afternoons, and comfortable evenings, making it ideal for safaris. The park remains closed from May to September due to the scorching heat and monsoon rains, which make wildlife sightings challenging and uncomfortable for tourists.

The peak tourist season, from November to March, offers the best opportunities for spotting the majestic Royal Bengal tigers, leopards, and a wide variety of bird species. However, if you're looking for a more relaxed atmosphere and fewer tourists, consider visiting the park during the shoulder season (April to May or October to November).

**How to Reach**

Bandhavgarh National Park is situated in the heart of Madhya Pradesh, approximately 200 km (124 miles) from Jabalpur, the nearest airport. The park is connected to major Indian cities through various means:

1. **By Air:** The nearest airport is Jabalpur Airport (JLR), which is well-connected to cities like Delhi (BOM, DEL), Mumbai (BOM), and Ahmedabad (AMD). From the airport, take a taxi or bus to Umaria (approximately 100 km, 62 miles), followed by a road transfer to the park.
2. **By Train:** Umaria Railway Station (URM) is approximately 30 km (19 miles) from the park's entry ticket counter. Trains are available from major cities like Mumbai (BCT), Delhi (HBJ), and Bhopal (BPL). From Umaria Railway Station, take a taxi or bus to the park.
3. **By Road:** Bandhavgarh is connected to major cities through highways. Take a taxi, bus, or self-drive to Tala or Umaria from Jabalpur, Khajuraho, or other nearby cities. From Tala or Umaria, take a private vehicle to the park's entry ticket counter.

**Top Places to Visit**

Bandhavgarh National Park is a treasure trove of natural wonders and wildlife sightings. Some top places to visit within the park include:

1. **Fort of Bandhavgarh (Bhadbhadh)**: A 16th-century ancient fort perched atop a hill, the Fort offers panoramic views of the surrounding landscape and the National Park.
2. **Bandhavgarh National Park (Tala)**: Explore the park's rich wildlife, spotting iconic tigers, leopards, and a variety of bird species.
3. **Kanha National Park**: One of the most renowned national parks in India, Kanha offers stunning scenery, diverse wildlife, and a serene atmosphere.
4. **Khandari Wildlife Sanctuary**: A relatively lesser-known sanctuary, Khandari is a habitat for a variety of flora and fauna, including spotted deer, peacocks, and monkeys.

**Travel Tips**

To make the most of your trip to Bandhavgarh National Park:

1. **Book your accommodations in advance**: Ensure you have a comfortable place to stay during your visit.
2. **Hire an experienced guide**: A local guide can help you spot the majestic tigers and other wildlife.
3. **Respect wildlife and the environment**: Follow guidelines to minimize your impact on the park's ecosystem.
4. **Stay hydrated**: The weather in the park can be scorching. Don't forget to carry enough water and snack.
5. **Capture breathtaking memories**: Bring a camera to capture stunning moments of wildlife and scenic vistas.

**Budget Tips**

To save on your Bandhavgarh National Park trip, follow these budget-friendly tips:

1. **Opt for budget accommodations**: Choose cost-effective accommodations like guesthouses or eco-lodges.
2. **Hire a local guide or join a group tour**: Group tours often result in lower costs and better access to park guides.
3. **Pack light and avoid unnecessary expenses**: Bring essential items to avoid excessive luggage and unnecessary expenses.
4. **Book your tickets in advance**: Ensure you get the best rates for park entries and other activities.
5. **Eat local cuisine**: Enjoy local flavors and cuisine to stay within your budget.
