
# Mount Abu travel guide

**Discover the Serene Bliss of Mount Abu: A Travel Guide**

Located in the Aravalli hills of Rajasthan, Mount Abu is India's only hill station at an altitude of 1,300 meters above sea level. It is a tranquil oasis surrounded by lush green forests, breathtaking waterfalls, and serene lakes. This enchanting hill station has been a favorite among tourists and locals alike for centuries, offering a unique opportunity to experience the charm of nature, rich cultural heritage, and a relaxing getaway from the hustle and bustle of city life.

**Best Time to Visit**

The best time to visit Mount Abu is from September to April, when the climate is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing and outdoor activities. During this period, the mercury hovers between 10°C to 25°C, making it perfect for exploring the hills, enjoying waterfalls, and witnessing the majestic beauty of the Aravalli hills. The monsoon season from May to August is also an excellent time to visit, but it may not be suitable for tourists who prefer dry conditions.

**How to Reach**

Mount Abu is well-connected by road, rail, and air, making it easily accessible from anywhere in India.

- **By Air:** The nearest airport is Udaipur Airport, which is approximately 165 km away from Mount Abu. From Udaipur Airport, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Mount Abu.
- **By Rail:** The closest railway station is Abu Road Railway Station, which is about 28 km away from Mount Abu. From Abu Road, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Mount Abu.
- **By Road:** You can also drive to Mount Abu from major cities in India, such as Ahmedabad, Jaipur, and Delhi, via NH-8 and NH-58.

**Top Places to Visit**

Mount Abu is home to some of the most breathtaking and historically significant attractions in the region.

1. **Dilwara Temples:** A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Dilwara Temples are a majestic example of the Rajput and Jain architectural styles. The temples are carved out of intricately decorated marble and feature some of the finest examples of intricate craftsmanship.
2. **Nakki Lake:** A tranquil lake surrounded by the majestic hills, Nakki Lake is an ideal spot for boating, fishing, and enjoying the serene beauty of nature.
3. **Doodh Baori:** A beautiful stepwell, Doodh Baori is a masterpiece of ancient architecture and engineering. The stepwell is carved out of a single block of marble and features a breathtaking entrance that is reminiscent of ancient India.
4. **Sunset Point:** This stunning viewpoint offers panoramic views of the Aravalli hills and the sunset, making it a perfect spot to capture memories.
5. ** Trevor's Tank:** A tranquil spot surrounded by lush green forests, Trevor's Tank is an ideal place for birdwatching and relaxing in nature.
6. **Achalgarh Fort:** This 14th-century fort is a masterpiece of Rajput architecture and offers panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys.

**Travel Tips**

Before you plan your trip to Mount Abu, here are some essential travel tips to keep in mind:

- **Weather:** Mount Abu is a hill station and has a cool climate, especially during the night. Pack accordingly to stay warm and comfortable.
- **Clothing:** Pack comfortable and lightweight clothing for your trip. Avoid heavy and tight-fitting clothes.
- **Food:** Mount Abu has plenty of restaurants and cafes serving a variety of cuisines, including local Rajasthani and Indian dishes. Try the local cuisine and drinks to experience the authentic taste of India.
- **Language:** Hindi and Gujarati are the local languages spoken in Mount Abu. However, English is widely spoken, making it easy for tourists to communicate and navigate.

**Budget Tips**

Here are some budget tips to help you save money during your trip to Mount Abu:

- **Accommodation:** Book your hotel or resort in advance to get the best rates. Consider staying at a guesthouse or homestay for a more affordable option.
- **Food:** Eat at local restaurants and cafes to save money. Opt for local cuisine and drinks to experience the authentic taste of India.
- **Transportation:** Use public transport or hire a taxi for a shorter distance. Ask your hotel for recommendations on the best transportation options.
- **Activities:** Opt for free activities like visiting the Nakki Lake, Trevor's Tank, and the Sunset Point. Pay for guided tours for attractions like the Dilwara Temples and the Achalgarh Fort.

**Conclusion**

Mount Abu is a serene hill station that offers a unique opportunity to experience the charm of nature, rich cultural heritage, and a relaxing getaway from the hustle and bustle of city life. With its breathtaking waterfalls, tranquil lakes, and majestic hills, Mount Abu is an ideal destination for tourists and nature lovers alike. Whether you are interested in exploring the ancient architecture, experiencing the local cuisine, or simply relaxing in nature, Mount Abu has something to offer for everyone. So, plan your trip to Mount Abu today and discover the serene bliss of this enchanting hill station.
