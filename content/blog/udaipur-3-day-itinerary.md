
# Udaipur 3 day itinerary

**Discover the Gem of Rajasthan: A 3-Day Udaipur Itinerary**

Tucked away in the scenic landscape of Rajasthan, India, lies the City of Lakes, Udaipur. This enchanting city is a treasure trove of rich history, stunning architecture, and breathtaking natural beauty. In this blog, we'll delve into the best time to visit, how to reach, and create a 3-day itinerary to explore the top places to visit in Udaipur.

**Best Time to Visit**

The best time to visit Udaipur is during the winter months (October to March), when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing. The city experiences a dry climate, with temperatures ranging from 10°C to 25°C (50°F to 77°F). This period is perfect for outdoor activities, boat rides, and exploring the city's many attractions.

**How to Reach**

Getting to Udaipur is relatively easy, with multiple transportation options available:

1. **By Air**: Udaipur has its own airport, which receives flights from major cities like Delhi, Mumbai, and Jaipur. You can also take connecting flights from other cities.
2. **By Train**: Udaipur is well-connected to other major cities in India by train. You can take a train from Delhi, Jaipur, or Mumbai to Udaipur.
3. **By Bus**: Bus services are also available from nearby cities like Jaipur, Jodhpur, and Ahmedabad.

**Top Places to Visit**

Udaipur is a treasure trove of stunning palaces, beautiful gardens, and sacred temples. Here are the top places to visit in Udaipur:

1. **City Palace**: A majestic complex of palaces, courtyards, and museums, City Palace is one of the most popular attractions in Udaipur. The palace is a must-visit to explore its rich history and stunning architecture.
2. **Lake Pichola**: A serene and picturesque lake, Lake Pichola is a perfect spot for a romantic boat ride. The lake is surrounded by stunning palaces and temples, making it a photographer's paradise.
3. **Jag Mandir**: A beautiful palace built on an island in Lake Pichola, Jag Mandir is a stunning example of Rajasthani architecture. Take a boat ride to the palace and explore its beautiful gardens and courtyards.
4. **Sahelion Ki Bari**: A beautiful garden built for the ladies of the royal family, Sahelion Ki Bari is a tranquil oasis in the heart of the city. Take a leisurely walk through the garden and admire its stunning fountains and sculptures.
5. **Monsoon Palace**: A hilltop palace built by the late Maharana of Mewar, Monsoon Palace offers breathtaking views of the city and Lake Pichola. Take a scenic drive to the palace and watch the sunset over the lake.

**Day-wise Itinerary**

Here's a suggested 3-day itinerary to explore the top places to visit in Udaipur:

### Day 1: Explore the City Palace and Lake Pichola

1. Start your day by visiting the City Palace, a majestic complex of palaces, courtyards, and museums.
2. Take a leisurely walk around the palace grounds and explore the beautiful gardens and courtyards.
3. Spend the afternoon exploring the city's vibrant markets and shopping for souvenirs.
4. End your day with a romantic boat ride on Lake Pichola, watching the sunset over the lake.

### Day 2: Visit the Jag Mandir and Monsoon Palace

1. Start your day by taking a boat ride to the Jag Mandir, a stunning palace built on an island in Lake Pichola.
2. Explore the palace and its beautiful gardens, and learn about its rich history.
3. Take a scenic drive to the Monsoon Palace, a hilltop palace that offers breathtaking views of the city and Lake Pichola.
4. Spend the evening exploring the city's nightlife and trying local cuisine.

### Day 3: Visit the Sahelion Ki Bari and Explore the City

1. Start your day by visiting the Sahelion Ki Bari, a beautiful garden built for the ladies of the royal family.
2. Take a leisurely walk through the garden and admire its stunning fountains and sculptures.
3. Spend the afternoon exploring the city's many temples and mosques, and learning about its rich cultural heritage.
4. End your day with a scenic drive to the outskirts of the city, where you can enjoy stunning views of the surrounding countryside.

**Travel Tips**

1. **Best Time to Visit**: Udaipur is best visited during the winter months (October to March) when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing.
2. **Clothing**: Pack light and breathable clothing for your trip to Udaipur, and don't forget to bring sunscreen and a hat to protect yourself from the sun.
3. **Food**: Don't miss trying local cuisine, which includes delicious dishes like dal bati churma and ghevar. Try local restaurants and street food stalls to experience the authentic flavors of Udaipur.
4. **Transportation**: Hire a local taxi or auto rickshaw to get around the city, and use public transportation to save money.

**Budget Tips**

1. **Accommodation**: Book your accommodations in advance to save money, and opt for budget-friendly options like guesthouses or hostels.
2. **Food**: Eat at local restaurants and street food stalls to save money, and try to avoid expensive touristy restaurants.
3. **Transportation**: Use public transportation to save money, and avoid hiring taxis or auto rickshaws for long distances.
4. **Entry Fees**: Be prepared to pay entry fees for most attractions in Udaipur, and plan your itinerary accordingly.

**Conclusion**

Udaipur is a stunning city that has something to offer for every kind of traveler. From its breathtaking palaces and gardens to its stunning lakes and scenic drives, Udaipur is a treasure trove of natural beauty and cultural heritage. This 3-day itinerary provides a comprehensive guide to exploring the top places to visit in Udaipur, and offers valuable tips and insights to help you plan your trip. So pack your bags, grab your camera, and get ready to discover the gem of Rajasthan – Udaipur.
