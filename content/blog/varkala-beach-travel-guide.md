
# Varkala beach travel guide

**Discover the Hidden Gem of Kerala: Varkala Beach Travel Guide**

Tucked away in the southwestern coast of India, Varkala Beach is a tranquil haven that has been a favorite among travelers and beach lovers for centuries. This picturesque seaside town in the state of Kerala, also known as the "God's Own Country," is a treasure trove of natural beauty, rich history, and vibrant culture. In this comprehensive travel guide, we'll delve into the must-visit places, the best time to explore, and expert tips to make your Varkala Beach vacation an unforgettable experience.

**Introduction**

Located about 51 km north of Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of Kerala, Varkala Beach is a small but significant tourist destination. This beach town boasts a stunning 1.6-kilometer-long beach with towering red laterite cliffs, pristine waters, and powdery white sand. The tranquil atmosphere and serene landscape of Varkala Beach make it an ideal getaway from the hustle and bustle of city life.

**Best Time to Visit**

The best time to visit Varkala Beach is during the winter months, from October to February. This period offers pleasant weather with moderate temperatures, ranging from 20°C to 25°C (68°F to 77°F). The summer months, from March to May, are warm and sunny, while the monsoon season, from June to September, brings heavy rainfall and rough seas. If you're a thrill-seeker, the monsoon season can be a great time to visit Varkala Beach, as the rough seas make it an ideal spot for surfing.

**How to Reach**

Reaching Varkala Beach is relatively easy, and you can choose from various modes of transportation:

1. **By Air**: The nearest airport is Trivandrum International Airport (TRV), which is about 51 km from Varkala Beach. You can take a taxi or bus from the airport to reach Varkala Beach.
2. **By Train**: Varkala Railway Station (VAK) is the nearest railway station, which is about 3 km from the beach. You can take a train from any major city in India to Trivandrum and then take a taxi or bus to Varkala Beach.
3. **By Bus**: Regular bus services are available from Trivandrum, Thiruvalla, and other nearby towns to Varkala Beach.
4. **By Private Car**: You can hire a private car or taxi from Trivandrum or other nearby towns to reach Varkala Beach.

**Top Places to Visit**

Varkala Beach is a treasure trove of attractions, and here are some of the top places to visit:

1. **Papanasam Beach**: Located adjacent to Varkala Beach, Papanasam Beach is a scenic spot with a calm and peaceful atmosphere.
2. **Sree Janardana Swamy Temple**: This ancient temple is dedicated to Lord Krishna and is a significant Hindu pilgrimage site.
3. **Varkala Tunnel**: This 180-foot-long tunnel is a great spot for photography and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
4. **Sarovaram Bio Park**: This wildlife sanctuary is a great place to spot a variety of bird species and other wildlife.
5. **Kappil Beach**: Located about 4 km from Varkala Beach, Kappil Beach is a scenic spot with a beautiful beach and calm waters.
6. **Varkala Lighthouse**: This 30-meter-tall lighthouse offers panoramic views of the surrounding landscape and is a great spot for photography.
7. **Chilicoor Waterfalls**: Located about 10 km from Varkala Beach, Chillicoor Waterfalls is a scenic spot with stunning waterfalls and a calm atmosphere.

**Travel Tips**

Here are some expert travel tips to help you make the most of your Varkala Beach vacation:

1. **Best Transportation**: Rent a scooter or motorcycle to explore the nearby attractions.
2. **Accommodation**: Choose a budget-friendly hotel or guesthouse near the beach.
3. **Food**: Try local cuisine, including fresh fish, coconut-based dishes, and traditional Kerala delicacies.
4. **Beach Safety**: Be cautious while swimming in the sea, as the currents can be strong.
5. **Cultural Sensitivity**: Respect local customs and traditions, especially when visiting temples and cultural sites.
