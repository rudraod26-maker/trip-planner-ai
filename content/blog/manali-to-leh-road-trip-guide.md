
# Manali to Leh road trip guide

**The Unparalleled Beauty of the Himalayas: A Manali to Leh Road Trip Guide**

Are you ready for the ultimate adventure in one of the world's most breathtaking landscapes? **Introduction**

Manali to Leh road trip is an iconic journey that spans over 480 kilometers and can be completed in 5-7 days, depending on your schedule and preferences. This road trip takes you through the rugged terrain of the Himalayas, offering breathtaking views of snow-capped mountains, picturesque villages, and serene lakes. The trip begins from Manali, a scenic hill station in the state of Himachal Pradesh, and ends at Leh, a fascinating city in the state of Jammu and Kashmir.

**Best Time to Visit**

The best time to visit the Manali to Leh road trip is during the summer months of June to August, when the roads are open and the weather is pleasant. During this time, the temperatures range from 15°C to 25°C, making it ideal for trekking and other outdoor activities. However, it is essential to note that the roads can be closed due to heavy snowfall during the winter months.

**How to Reach**

The Manali to Leh road trip begins from Manali, which is connected to major cities like Delhi and Chandigarh through bus and train services. You can also hire a taxi or a bike to reach Manali. From Manali, you can hire a taxi or a bike that will take you to Leh through the Manali-Leh Highway (NH 21).
